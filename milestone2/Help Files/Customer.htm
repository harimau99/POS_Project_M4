Add New Customer
To add a new customer, click Add New Customer under Customer option in the Main Menu. The form displayed allows you to type in a new customer's details, eg. Customer Name, Customer ID Number, Contact Number etc. Click Add Customer button to save the new customer.
View All Customers
To view information of all customers, click View All Customers under Customer on the Main Menu. Displayed is all customer details in a GridView. To optionally update a customer, you may click on the customer record and then click the Update Customer Details button.
Update Customer
To update a customer's details, click on Update Customer under Customer in the Main Menu. The Update Customer form displayed will require you to either search for a customer by name or select a customer from the GridView. You may edit the data via the fields displayed and click the Update Customer Details button to save.
