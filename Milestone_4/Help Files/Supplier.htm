Add New Supplier
To add a new supplier, click on Add New Supplier under Supplier in the Main Menu. The form displayed requires you to enter the new supplier's information in the fields provided. Click on the Add New Supplier button to save.
View All Suppliers
To view all supplier details, click on View All Suppliers under Supplier in the Main Menu. Displayed is all supplier details in a GridView. You may optionally update a supplier's details by clicking on a supplier record and then click on Update Supplier Details button.
Update Supplier
To update a supplier's details, click on Update Supplier under Supplier in the Main Menu. The form displayed will require you to either search for a supplier by name or select a supplier from the GridView. You may edit the data via the fields displayed and click the Update Supplier Details button to save.
