Add New Product
To add a new product, click on Add New Product under Product in the Main Menu. The form displayed will require you to enter the product's details such as Product Name, Model, Quantity etc. To save the new product, click on the Add Product button.
View All Products
To view the information of all products, click on View All Products under Product in the Main Menu. Displayed is the details of all products in a GridView. You may optionally update a products information by clicking on the Update Product button.
Update Product
To update a products details, click on Update Produc under Product in the Main Menu. The Update Product form displayed will require you to either search for a product by name or select a product from the GridView. You may edit the data via the fields displayed and click on Update Product button to save.
