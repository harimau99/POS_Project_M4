Process Sale
To process a sale order, click on Process Sale under Sales in the Main Menu. Displayed is the Process Sale form whereby you may search for the customer by customer ID or customer name. Seach for products by product name to add to the customers' order. You may choose the delivery option for the customer by typing in their destination distance to calculate their delivery charge. Once the order is finalised, the products can then be added to the invoice by clicking Add To Invoice button. To finalise the sale, click on Finalise Sale button. Once payment has been received, you may record the payment by clicking the Record Payment button. To display and print out the invoice, click on the Get Invoice button.
View All Sales
To view all the sales processed through this system, click on View All Sales under Sales in the Main Menu. Displayed is the View All Sales form whereby you may search for a customer by name or ID to view their sale orders as well as the products they have purchased.
